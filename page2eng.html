I feel very inspired by stones.
In alot of things the matter stone comes along, especially in two extremes: nature & buildings.
Nature forming the stone and humans creating stone from earth.

In 2014-2015 I photographed my first series of black and white pictures of the Flemisch garages. I have done research in Bruges, where I could experience the purity of Flanders. What stands out the most is that in every conner of a street there is a garagebox. The search to all these hidden places was very interesting, I had found myself in places where I'd neven been before, and I know alot of people will never get to see. Those hidden places had such a charm and beautiful character.Actually, for the people who don't have a garage, it is forbidden to be there. The interesting fact is that, now, I can show those places to people. 

In 2015-2016 I made the same research in Ghent but I made the pictures in color. With the same thought I went on my little trips, on my search to garageboxes. 

I think it's very fascinating, I will keep on making photographs.

I take for granted that there are autoboxes in Wallonie, maybe they have simularities or maybe they are very diferent from the garages in Flanders?

I think that will be my new research?
