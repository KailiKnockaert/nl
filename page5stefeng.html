Life

Stefaan Vanfleten was born in Kortrijk in 1969, he is one of the most known Belgium photografers all over the world. He studied at Sint-Lucas in Brussels. He usually works in black and white, with his work he tries to sketch an image of Belgium. The hard life some people live rellay speak in his pictures.
Below you can see some of his photographs, he has been my inspiration to get my camera more often with me, thanks to Stefaan Vanfleten I leran to look different to thinks in my region and capture them as they are. You can also see we have one thing in commen is to work in series.
